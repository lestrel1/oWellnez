About Us üåê

The mission of our website, created by Loraine Estrella De Leon as part of the CodePath Web Development Advocacy Project, is to empower individuals on their journey towards holistic wellness by providing comprehensive health resources and tools to help them take control of their lifestyle and well-being. We aim to educate, inspire, and guide our users towards making informed, positive choices for optimal mental, emotional, and physical health.


Our intended users are teenagers aged 13 and above, as well as anyone seeking to improve various aspects of their health and overall quality of life. Through our integrated platform, users can access a range of features, including personal journals (public or private), a community blog to share impactful stories, educational podcasts, informative videos, thought-provoking articles, and immersive wellness events - all designed to support our users' path to achieving their health and wellness goals.


As an advocacy project, our website is committed to promoting a culture of self-care, mindfulness, and proactive health management. By leveraging the power of digital resources and fostering a supportive community, we strive to empower our users to take charge of their well-being and inspire positive, sustainable changes in their lives.
